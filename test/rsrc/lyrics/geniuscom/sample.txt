            About “SONG”
          </h3>

      <div class="rich_text_formatting">
        <p>The fifth track on SAMPLE’s debut album, <a href="https://genius.com/albums/SAMPLE/ALBUM" rel="noopener" data-api_path="/albums/325258">ALBUM</a> is the most reminiscent of her previous ep’s with a pop-disco sound.</p>

<p>She reflects about her insecurities on not maturing as quickly as her partner is. <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/SONG" rel="noopener nofollow">SONG season</a> is usually the last big event before graduation for a high schooler, and SAMPLE is too busy living in the moment that she forgets she has her future to look forward to.</p>

<p><img src="#" alt="" width="1000" height="544" data-animated="false"></p>

<p>This is also around the age when friends are growing apart and have decided where and what they’re doing with their lives, and a lot are trying to hold on to the people they’ve known, even though they’re growing up differently. The person SAMPLE is talking to in the song seems to take everything, even change, to heart.</p>
